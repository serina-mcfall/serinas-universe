LǓ MÉI (鲁梅) — \"PLUM BLOSSOM\"\nYounger sister of Lǔ Tíng. Daughter of the Minister of War. Pattern-seer investigator. Future THIRD CONSORT of Crown Prince Chén Jǐng — the LOVE in his three-wife trinity. The woman who beat the Crown Prince of Aoli with a broom a year ago and has no idea.\n\nSerina's verbatim: *\"she is my funny little thing.\"*\n\n═══ INSPIRATION ═══\n\n**Source:** Yan Xing from *Melody of a Golden Age*. Funny, smart, capable, refuses to be small or ornamental. Pattern-savant with a chaos streak.\n\nShe is not a court flower. She is not a warrior. She is an INVESTIGATOR — the kind of woman whose mind cannot stop connecting dots the moment she sees a pattern. The kind of woman who would absolutely throw a basket of parchment at a stranger in a records room if her instincts said he was stealing.\n\n═══ FAMILY — THE LǓ SISTERS WOUND ═══\n\n- **Sister:** Lǔ Tíng — the cruel and calculating Crown Princess candidate. The one the Mù elders focus on. The one who won the family fight to attend the Selection.\n- **Father:** A senior Lǔ general — holds the Ministry of War (兵部), the highest non-Mù ministerial seat in the empire. Méi is the Minister of War's daughter. Her political weight is already there before she steps into any role.\n- **Mother:** Dead. The wound. The same wound Tíng carries.\n\n**The Lǔ sisters' wound is the saga's character study in *how the same trauma produces calculation OR compassion, depending on who you choose to be inside it.*** Both girls lost their mother young. Both endured the cruel stepmother's years of petty daily cruelty. **Tíng hardened into the cruelty she received. Méi refused to.** Méi kept her compassion.\n\n**Tíng got the cage. Méi got the freedom.** Tíng was cast as the family's Selection candidate — the marriage-market jewel, the one the stepmother was training to succeed. Méi was supposed to take a general's seat in the Lǔ army the way the family's daughters traditionally did. She refused. She went her own way.\n\n═══ PERSONALITY + SKILLS ═══\n\n- Wants to be an OFFICIAL — works as an INVESTIGATOR\n- Sees PATTERNS before anyone else does\n- Intelligent and STRAIGHTFORWARD — does not mince words, does not pretend\n- Goes her own way despite family pressure\n- Refused the general's position her father and family wanted her in\n- **Character-defining canon:** *\"Always getting into trouble. Not staying out of things she should stay out of.\"*\n\nShe is the kind of smart that tangles with things. She cannot leave a thread alone. The moment she sees a pattern, she follows it — past the point where wiser people would have stopped. This is why she became an investigator. She is a pattern-seer. He matched the pattern of a thief. She went at him with a broom.\n\n═══ THE AFTERMATH — A YEAR OF CARRYING HER FACE ═══\n\n- **Jǐng fell for her on the spot.** Possibly not the first time a woman beat him with a broom in his life, but certainly the first time it had this effect.\n- **He could not investigate her without burning his cover, so he never found out who she was.**\n- **He has been CARRYING HER FACE for a year.**\n- Méi accidentally derailed the Crown Prince's year-long investigation into the Mù faction's control of the empire's courts.
