Braedun tree is the giant ancient tree at the centre of the Sacred Grove — one of eight pillars holding the world in shape. If the corruption reaches it, one of the pillars falls.\n\nTHE EIGHT VIRTUES\n\nEach of the eight sacred trees holds one sacred virtue at its heart. Serina's verbatim from the prologue: *\"Each held something sacred within its heart: joy, memory, mercy, courage, wisdom, time, hope, and change.\"*\n\nThe eight virtues in the order Serina named them: JOY, MEMORY, MERCY, COURAGE, WISDOM, TIME, HOPE, CHANGE. Each of the eight trees holds ONE of these virtues. Each country of Avalúne therefore carries an ETHICAL AXIS — the virtue its sacred tree was planted to guard. **Tree-virtue assignments are PENDING** — Serina to lock which virtue belongs to which tree/country when she returns to this work.\n\nBound back in the prologue's closing world-state: *\"Hope is traded for bread, and mercy for coin.\"* Two of the eight virtues named explicitly as the things being LOST in the current age.
