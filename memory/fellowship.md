## The Fellowship

Six outcasts bound by a waking world and questions nobody else will answer. Like a fellowship (LOTR style) but bound not by a ring but by prophecy and shared searching. Alone they're outcasts; together they're the answer.

### 1. Aria Tyramare -- The Builder (Age 16)
- **Role:** The prophecy child. The centre. The spark.
- **Origin:** Born in Aurumshi. Orphan. No knowledge of her parents.
- **Parents:** Father is Zhuohuo (Bannric heir with Fengzhe inside). Mother is Minhui (spirit fox goddess).
- **Bloodlines:** Carries blood of BOTH sleeping gods (through the immortal twins), Bannric royal blood, fox spirit, and phoenix fire. ALL five elements.
- **Appearance:** Auburn hair, freckles, green eyes that sparkle with inner light. Pretty, not beautiful. Wild hair.
- **Personality:** Spunky, funny, cheeky. Really smart. Remarkable artificer who can build anything. Asks too many questions. Has dyslexia and AuDHD. The blood magic fog in Aurumshi slides off her because her brain refuses to be smoothed down.
- **Her Bird:** A mechanical bird she built -- her best friend. A bit of magic seeped into it. It hears the rivers, mountains, wind. It whispers to her: "Patience. The time is soon."
- **Weapons:**
  - **Hidden daggers** -- left at the orphanage by Minhui. Made from Minhui's own spirit. Transparent crystal. When used, they spread out like a fan and dance on the air. They're solidified divine spirit.
  - **A gun** she built herself -- powered by elemental magic. Shoots condensed bullets of whatever element she channels (fire bullet = condensed fire magic).
  - **The bird** -- also a weapon/tool.
- **Goal:** Get off the sky city. Travel to earth. Explore the world below.
- **Family name:** Tyramare -- Minhui gave her the name of the province where the empty throne sits.

### 2. Yue'an -- The Shadow (Age 20, the eldest)
- **Role:** The weapon who chose love. Aria's opposite. Her love interest.
- **Origin:** Created by Yueli in the Shi Sect. He is Yueli's SON -- her answer to Mujin's Aria.
- **Appearance:** Striking, almost inhuman beauty. East Asian features. Pale luminous skin like moonlight on marble. Sharp angular features. Dark intense eyes. Ornamental mark between brows (a seal). Long black hair with silver-blue threads. Black robes with silver-gold embroidery.
- **Personality:** Silent, reserved, cold. Always watching. Always calculating. Fights the darkness inside him. He carries a fragment of Yueli -- he fights the hunger. Hates the darkness at first, then learns to cherish it, then tries to protect it.
- **Name:** Moon Shadow. Styles himself the Shadow Prince of Maramir.
- **Powers:** All five elements (like Aria, but was not meant to be). Master of illusion and mirage. Assassin.
- **Weapons:** Shadow blades conjured from space itself. Dark as night. No reflection. They absorb light. Each time he uses them, his SHADOW GROWS bigger, darker, more present. The more darkness he uses, the larger the shadow around him gets.
- **Key relationship:** Aria quiets his hunger with LIGHT. Genmu quiets it with HEALING. He and Aria will hate each other at first, then fall in love.

### 3. Xuehua -- The Trickster (Age 16-17)
- **Role:** The spy. The fox. The one who knows everyone's secrets.
- **Origin:** Fengyu/Wen Sect (wind sect). A four-tailed fox cultivator working toward nine.
- **Appearance:** Three forms:
  - **Full human:** Black hair with white and pink strands (like cherry blossoms in ink). Golden amber eyes. Seductive, mischievous. Red lips, red nails.
  - **Half-fox:** Between human and fox. White robes, fox ears and tail visible.
  - **Full fox:** Pure white with four pink-tipped tails (like cherry blossoms on fresh snow).
- **Personality:** Total trickster. Seductive, flirty, nosy. A character. Takes after Wenji. Walks into a room and every head turns. Asks innocent questions and twenty minutes later you've told her your deepest secret.
- **Name meaning:** Snow flower. Cherry blossoms in winter. Life and death. Spring and winter.
- **Key relationship:** Has a deep fox instinct to protect Genmu -- she senses he's sacred/divine.

### 4. Genmu -- The Heart (Age 14, the youngest)
- **Role:** The healer. The gentle one. The one who FEELS.
- **Origin:** Shanshi/Yan Sect (earth sect). Connected to the divine bloodline of Yanlong and Yunsheng.
- **Appearance:** Young, soft features. Big dark eyes. Colourful embroidered robes in earth tones, teals, reds. Barely more than a boy.
- **Personality:** Healer of the land. Small in stature. Feels deeply. Trees whisper to him, flowers sing, the land LOVES him. Wears his heart on his sleeve. Hurts easily.
- **Key relationships:** Aria is protective (like a big sister). Xuehua's fox instincts compel her to protect him. He quiets the darkness in Yue'an through healing (not fighting the darkness but MENDING it). Yue'an will learn to cherish and protect him.

### 5. Temgar -- The Iron Spear (Age 18-19)
- **Role:** The big brother. The muscle. The warmth.
- **Origin:** Isenholt. Born of an ivyless marriage (outcast). Ice-blue eyes. Bonded to direwolf Meila.
- **Appearance:** Burly, full of life and vigor. Mix of Mongolian and Scandinavian features. Dark hair, wild. Ice-blue eyes that match Meila's. Battle scars. Fur and leather and bronze.
- **Personality:** Big, loud, ALIVE. Fills a room by walking into it. Laughs and you feel it in your chest. But also sharp and observant. Amazing sense of smell (better than Xuehua's). Cunning but not calculating -- sees a lot.
- **His Wolf:** Meila (name sounds like wind across snow). He found her as a pup in the wild, nursed her back to health. Ice-blue eyes match his. This bond is special -- his eye colour is unprecedented (not standard blue/amber/green).
- **Weapons:** Two swords on back. Sword in hand. Axes.
- **Key detail:** He was cast out -- outcasts don't get the bonding rite. He should never have had Meila. His ice-blue eyes set him apart and are one reason he was cast out.

### 6. Liyin Aurelian -- The Golden Shadow (Age 17, "going on 40")
- **Role:** The performer. The spy master. The smartest person in the room disguised as the loudest.
- **Origin:** Solisopia. Grew up in a pleasure house. Raised by the Water Twin (madam of the house) and the other women.
- **Appearance:** Lavender and gold robes. Braids, jewelled hair pieces. Red sash. Slight smirk. Beautiful and he KNOWS it.
- **Personality:** Flamboyant, loud, extroverted. Puts on a show. Hides his calculating mind behind spectacle. Tyrion Lannister energy. Knows all the stories, knows all the secrets. Connections to every underground den. Spy network in Tyramare's guild city. Performer.
- **Name:** Liyin (Beautiful Shadow) + Aurelian (Golden One). Chinese and Roman in one breath -- Solisopia in its entirety.
- **Key detail:** The Water Twin has quietly guided him. Knows what's happening and where. Has eyes and ears in the guild city via his operative on the Stillhaven seat.

## Fellowship Dynamics

- Aria and Yue'an: enemies to lovers. Light and shadow. Sun and moon.
- Xuehua and Genmu: fox protects sacred earth child. Instinctual bond.
- Temgar: the big brother of the group. Heart and muscle.
- Liyin: the one who knows things. Information is his weapon.
- All of them are outcasts and searchers. They find each other because they're all looking for something the world doesn't want them to find.
