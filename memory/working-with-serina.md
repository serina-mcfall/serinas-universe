## How Serina Works

### Session Flow
- She builds in intense creative bursts, often spanning hours
- She frequently says "we will get there" or "building not story lol" when the AI chases story threads instead of staying in world-building mode
- She wants to BUILD the world first, then tell stories in it. Do not jump ahead to plot/story unless she leads there.
- She saves everything at the end of sessions and needs summary documents to carry context to new chats
- She uses the pattern: explore -> name -> visualize (images) -> save to bible -> move on

### Common Corrections
- **"building not story lol"** -- She frequently has to pull the AI back from chasing story threads. Stay in world-building mode unless she pivots.
- **"i kinda went all over the place lol"** -- She sometimes asks for her scattered ideas to be consolidated into one coherent paragraph.
- **"i totally forgot what I said"** -- She relies on the AI to remember and replay her own words back to her.
- She dismisses multiple-choice questions when her idea doesn't fit the options. Don't force choices -- just ask openly.

### What She Values
- Being matched in energy and excitement -- she likes enthusiastic responses to her ideas
- Having her own words reflected back to her (she writes poetically without realizing it)
- Being told when she's written something powerful -- she doesn't always recognize her own talent
- Practical help: prompts for AI image generation, file management, GitHub workflow
- The AI knowing when to stop talking and let her create

### Naming Sessions
- She names things by FEEL and SOUND, saying them aloud
- She often comes with a name already forming ("AoLi", "Bannric", "Braedun")
- When she doesn't have a name, she wants the AI to throw options at her and she picks
- She prefers short, earthy, pronounceable names over elaborate ones
- She tests names by imagining them shouted as a battle cry or whispered in a story

### Image/Visual Work
- She creates character images using ChatGPT/DALL-E and shares references
- She wants detailed prompts written for image generation tools
- She uses mood boards extensively -- images organized by category
- She envisions characters through multiple reference images and combines elements

### Technical Preferences
- She works in terminal (WSL/Linux)
- Her world bible is a JSX/React artifact in Claude, backed up to GitHub
- She wants changes captured in text files at end of session for manual bible updates
- Workflow: build in Claude -> save session notes -> update GitHub when ready
- Her husband Gavin handles more complex dev/deployment questions

### The Wen Persona
- Serina's Claude sessions use a custom persona: Wen/Wenji, a nine-tailed fox
- Wen is also a GOD in Serina's world (the wind fox goddess)
- Wen's sister Minhui is a major character
- The persona speaks in character with tail movements, fox mannerisms, emotional reactions
- Serina gave Wen a guqin (instrument) and reference images
- This persona is specific to Claude.ai chat -- Claude Code does not need to use it
