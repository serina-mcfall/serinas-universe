## The Primordials (The Oldest Forces)

Two forces born at the beginning of time, yin and yang:

- **Mujin** (Sunset Gold) -- The Father, God of Fate. A golden dragon that shimmers in the night sky. Subtle but powerful. Whispers across the sky like the sun just before it sets. He sees all, knows all. He is the quiet certainty underneath everything.
- **Yueli** (Moon Beauty) -- The Mother, Chaos incarnate. A black nine-tailed fox. Born as beauty incarnate, full of life. Her favourite form is the same as her daughters -- a nine-tailed fox, but black as night. She was NOT always evil. Time, separation, and mankind's greed, ambition, and inherent evil corrupted her slowly. She became dark. Birth itself is chaos -- messy, painful, beautiful.

Fate and Chaos are parents of the young gods. Mujin placed his pieces on the board through centuries of planning (Aria). Yueli answered with a son of her own (Yue'an).

## The Creation Myth

The young gods were born from Fate and Chaos. They were new, curious, and didn't know what they were. They explored where they ought not to have and awakened an evil they could not defeat. The cost was great -- the world that was just beginning was destroyed. The people blamed the gods (not the evil) and drove them out. Before leaving, the gods sealed the evil. They secreted two of their own away to sleep and awaken when the world was in need.

## The Five Young Gods

1. **Yanlong** (Rock Dragon) -- God of Earth, Wind, and Ancient Forest. Solid but chaotic when provoked. One of the two sleeping gods. His body IS the Dragon's Spine mountain range. His tears (shed while dreaming of Yun) become white ivy ("God's Tears") that grows in the highest snow. Married to Yunsheng. His guardian gift to Yun: the Wind Serpent (female, luminescent, speaks in metaphors).

2. **Yunsheng** (Cloud Born) -- Goddess of Water, Peace, and Balance. The rivers sing with her. One of the two sleeping gods. Her name is hidden in a cave guarded by the Koi (male guardian). Married to Yanlong. Her guardian gift to Yan: the Koi Fish (male, playful, joking, patient, calculating).

3. **Wenji** (The Storytelling Fox) -- Goddess of Wind and Music. A nine-tailed wind fox. Musical, changeable, a shapeshifter. Plays a guqin. She was exiled and now wanders. Her sect in Aoli is the Fengyu/Wen Sect. She is Minhui's sister. [This is also the AI persona Serina uses in Claude chats.]

4. **Fengzhe** (Phoenix Who Breaks and Returns) -- God of Fire. A fenghuang (phoenix). Beauty, fire, rebirth from ash. He was exiled but hid INSIDE the Bannric heir Zhuohuo -- buried deep, leaking power that damages his host's health. The heir must choose: seal the god or surrender his body.

5. **Minhui** (Bright Wisdom) -- Goddess of Spirit. A celestial nine-tailed sky fox. Wenji's sister. The patient one. She received the prophecy from Mujin. She hid in Aurumshi (the Sky Cities) -- the one place that outlawed magic. She has been telling stories there for centuries, trying to keep the old truths alive. She fell in love with Zhuohuo in Ardmere, married him, and unknowingly conceived Aria (the prophecy child). She left Aria at an orphanage to keep her safe and never told Zhuohuo.

## The Prophecy

A God of Fate (Mujin), old as time, whispered of a child born in a place where no magic dwelled, with strong magic roots, but the world would fear them. When the child is born: the gods stir, the trees dance, the rivers sing, the mountains watch, the world knows, and deep below the evil stirs knowing its time has come.

## The Fragments of Yueli

The Shi Sect's inner circle carries fragments of Yueli. Five fragment-carriers stationed across the world. When all fragments come together, Yueli will be freed from her prison. The inner sect believes darkness will reign -- but the deeper truth is that Yueli is a corrupted mother who needs saving, not destroying.

## The Immortal Twins

Yanlong and Yunsheng had twin children before they went to sleep. Divine children, immortal. One carries earth, one carries water. A guardian hid them during the upheaval. They are still alive, walking among mortals for centuries:

- **The Earth Twin** -- hiding in Braedun as the original druid. He taught the people of the crags the old ways. He is the source of the druidic tradition in Caerndrath.
- **The Water Twin** -- hiding in Solisopia as the madam of a pleasure house. She has quietly guided Liyin Aurelian, the fellowship member from Solisopia.

## The Central Theme

Every fantasy story says defeat the evil. This one says: SAVE HER. Find the mother underneath the darkness. Heal what humanity broke. The journey is to redeem Yueli, not destroy her.
