## Naming Patterns by Culture

### Aoli (Chinese-inspired)
- Names are tonal, flowing, meaningful in Chinese
- Gods: Yanlong, Yunsheng, Wenji, Fengzhe, Minhui
- Primordials: Mujin (sunset gold), Yueli (moon beauty)
- Sects (short): Yan, Yun, Wen, Fen, Hun, Shi
- Sects (full): Shanshi, Shuijing, Fengyu, Fenghui, Hunzhi, Shimo
- Characters: Zhuohuo, Xuehua, Genmu, Yue'an

### Caerndrath (Celtic/Germanic/Norse)
- Hard consonants, rough edges, earthy. Sounds like swords and stone.
- Kingdom: Caerndrath (cairn + wrath)
- Dynasty: Bannric (bann + ric = horn/peak + ruler)
- Provinces: Braedun, Isenholt, Ardmere, Tyramare, Maramir
- Called "Houses" (House of Bannric)

### Isenholt (Scandinavian/Mongolian blend)
- Compound words, earthy, simple. Names a warrior would shout or whisper.
- Clans: Greymane, Mistborne, Stonewall, Shroudborn, Skycrest, Coldhollow
- Warbands: Frost Howl, Vanishing Snow, Ironhide, Mistwalkers, Frostwing, Nightfall
- Leaders: Stoneborn, Stonebred, Fenrir, Ice Bear, Ghost of the Mountain, Winter Fox, Snow Talon, Wraith
- Titles: Hearthkeeper, Greysworn, Bonded, Silvermyst, Skyborn, Rootweaver, Volugan
- Characters: Temgar, Meila (his wolf)

### Tyramare (Mediterranean/Latin)
- Rolling, musical, with sharp edges. Like a lullaby hiding a blade.
- Districts: Spice Harbour, Marble Quarter, Iron Quarter, The Veil, Aurumvale, Stillhaven/The Silence Below, The Solace
- Faith: Solenthera (the faith), Entheras (the pope), Oranthi (temple runners)
- Cults: Umbrathi (Yueli's dark cult), Verenthae (Yunsheng's resistance)

### Solisopia (Roman/Byzantine fusion)
- Golden, rolling, excessive. Names drip like honey.
- Characters: Liyin Aurelian (Chinese + Roman in one name)

### Aurumshi (Latin + Chinese fusion)
- Aurum (Latin gold) + Shi (iron). West meets East.
- Characters: Aria Tyramare (Asian-English blend, family name from Caerndrath)
