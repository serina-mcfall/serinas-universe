## The Six Sects of Aoli

Each sect has a full ceremonial name and a short name used in daily speech. Short names are preferred.

### 1. Yan (Shanshi Sect) -- Mountain Stone / Earth
- **God:** Yanlong (Rock Dragon)
- **Location:** Carved INTO the Dragon's Spine itself. Buildings built into the mountain, blend of green and winter, frozen waterfalls, suspended in time.
- **Feel:** Grounded, steady, patient. The mountain doesn't move.
- **Connection:** ACTIVE -- their god sleeps right beneath them. They feel the heartbeat in the ground first.
- **How they sense the waking:** Feel the heartbeat in the ground.

### 2. Yun (Shuijing Sect) -- Water Stillness / Water
- **God:** Yunsheng (Cloud Born)
- **Location:** Smaller mountain ranges toward the valley. Perpetual spring. Cherry blossoms, flowers, Chinese maples, rivers and waterfalls.
- **Feel:** Peaceful on surface, immense power hidden below. Still water sees everything.
- **Connection:** ACTIVE -- their goddess sleeps in Aoli. They hear the rivers change.
- **How they sense the waking:** Hear the rivers sing. Aquatic animals feel the change.
- **Key:** Yan and Yun band together when the waking comes. They KNOW it's coming. The other four sects lost this knowledge when their gods were driven out.

### 3. Wen (Fengyu Sect) -- Wind Feather / Wind
- **God:** Wenji (Nine-tailed Wind Fox, the Storytelling Fox)
- **Location:** Above the clouds, closest to the heavens. Golden-white temples on pillar tops with sky above and cloud sea below.
- **Feel:** Musical, changeable, shapeshifting. Wind doesn't wait.
- **Connection:** SEVERED -- Wenji was exiled.
- **Cultivation gift:** Shapeshifting. Disciples can change into different animals depending on affinity and cultivation level. Don't pick your shape -- it picks you.
- **How they sense the waking:** Feel the air shift its tune.

### 4. Fen (Fenghui Sect) -- Phoenix Ash / Fire
- **God:** Fengzhe (The Phoenix Who Breaks and Returns)
- **Location:** Volcanic plains. Rivers of lava that flow like water but burn like fire. Cherry blossoms growing from ash. Compound glows gold from the earth beneath.
- **Feel:** Passion, destruction, transformation. Not about burning -- about what rises from the ash.
- **Connection:** SEVERED -- Fengzhe is hiding inside Zhuohuo.
- **How they sense the waking:** Feel it in their blood -- a stirring, suddenly MORE.

### 5. Hun (Hunzhi Sect) -- Soul Weave / Spirit
- **God:** Minhui (Celestial Sky Fox, Bright Wisdom)
- **Location:** Between cloud and earth. The sect closest to the people. Great halls wreathed in mist.
- **Feel:** The veil between life and death. Not a wall but a weave. Life and death are threads crossing.
- **Connection:** SEVERED -- Minhui hid in Aurumshi.
- **How they sense the waking:** Their souls sing.

### 6. Shi (Shimo Sect) -- Erode Into Oblivion / Chaos
- **God:** None officially. Actually serves Yueli through her avatar.
- **Location:** The Dark Range (southern mountains). Darkness everywhere, but signs of beauty in red maples. Winter never leaves.
- **Feel:** Never-ending pain. Hunger that has no bottom. Destruction at its base.
- **Connection:** Connected to Yueli's corruption. The hunger is their price of power.
- **How they sense the waking:** Hunger -- such a strong hunger for more.

## The Shimo Secret

The Shimo Sect has TWO layers:
- **Outer layer:** Cultivators who think chaos is just an element. They fight the hunger daily.
- **Inner layer:** A secret circle that KNOWS they serve Yueli. Led by Yueli's avatar -- her essence that took over the sect leader's conscience.

### The Five Fragment-Carriers (Inner Circle)

All young (preserved by fragments), shrouded in mystery. Faces covered by hoods or veils. Visual rule: women hide their eyes (veils, blindfolds), men hide faces from above (hoods, hats).

Each heads a discipline and is stationed across the world:

1. **The Avatar** -- Male. Head of the Shi Sect. Day-to-day leader. Does not hide who he is within the sect. The living proof of Yueli. Stationed in Aoli. Master of Corruption/Illusion magic.

2. **The Smiling Blade** -- Male. Beautiful, devious, deadly. Two swords on back, hidden knife. Smiles while he kills. Runs the pleasure quarter. Stationed in **Solisopia**. Master of Blades.

3. **The Blind Poisoner** -- Female. Blind, soft, cunning with deadly softness. Poison is her game. Poses as a travelling crone to pass messages. Stays in inner circle mostly. Master of Poisons.

4. **The Veiled Advisor** -- Female. Veiled in black, red, and gold. Advisor who whispers in ears of the ruling body. Practitioner of blood magic. Death disguised as beauty -- the sister who loves you, the mother who helps you. Stationed in **Aurumshi**. Master of Blood Magic.

5. **The Entheras** -- Male. Luxurious, fur-trimmed robes. Looks like a king, not a pope. Runs the Solenthera faith. Stationed in **Tyramare**. Master of Diplomacy. The "pope" figure -- the deciding vote on Tyramare's guild council. His seat is considered god-given; the public believes he is immortal by divine blessing.

### Five Disciplines of the Shi Sect
- **Poisons** (Blind Poisoner)
- **Blades** (Smiling Blade)
- **Diplomacy** (Entheras)
- **Blood Magic** (Veiled Advisor)
- **Corruption Magic** (Avatar) -- insidious magic that finds hairline cracks in the soul and feeds them

### Multi-Medium Potential
Each fragment-carrier could anchor their own story in a different medium/genre:
- Avatar in Aoli = xianxia cultivation story
- Smiling Blade in Solisopia = romance-laced thriller
- Entheras in Tyramare = political intrigue
- Veiled Advisor in Aurumshi = dark mystery
- Blind Poisoner = espionage/travel narrative
