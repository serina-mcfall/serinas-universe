## World Structure

One interconnected world with four distinct realms/cultures, each drawing from different real-world inspirations. The world holds multiple stories where characters can cross between tales.

## The Four Realms

### 1. Aoli (The Breathing Land)
- **Inspiration:** Chinese/xianxia, cultivation fiction, Asian history
- **Feel:** Sacred, alive. The air itself carries memory. Trees remember, mountains protect, wind whispers of old magic.
- **Geography:** Bordered by the Dragon's Spine (north, Yanlong's sleeping body) and the Dark Range (south, where Yueli's corruption is strongest). Between them: pillar forests, river valleys, terraced farmland, mist-wrapped peaks.
- **Borders:** Shares northern mountain border with Isenholt (two sides of same range)
- **Society:** Six cultivation sects, each tied to an element and a god
- **Magic:** Cultivation -- magic isn't something you USE, it's something you BECOME. Based on relationship with the land.
- **Key detail:** The land is alive -- a character in itself

### 2. Caerndrath (The Fractured Kingdom)
- **Inspiration:** Celtic, early European, Game of Thrones, Lord of the Rings
- **Feel:** Blood and iron. Old grudges. Crumbling glory with iron underneath.
- **Name origin:** Cairn (burial mounds) + wrath (anger that never burned out)
- **History:** Once a great empire ruled by the Bannric dynasty, destroyed from within by civil war. Now five fractured provinces, each ruled by a Bannric descendant. The throne has sat empty for centuries. Magic is hoarded by nobility; commoners with magic are hunted and killed.
- **The Lost Heir:** Rumours persist of the true Bannric heir who will one day unite the kingdom. This is Zhuohuo, who fled to Aoli.

#### Five Provinces of Caerndrath:

**Braedun** (The Wild Crags)
- Celtic inspiration. Riders like Rohan. Druidic magic hidden in plain sight.
- The people still feel the land even if they don't appreciate it. Stories of the old ways persist.
- The Earth Twin hid here, taught the druidic ways. Battle cry: "BRAEDUN!"
- Origin of druidic knowledge: An elder from Yanlong's sect (Shanshi) was tasked to cross to the crags. He fell in love, married, had children. The druidic knowledge was passed down through generations.
- When the noble purge of magic began, this druid family secreted their two young children away. One made it safely to the Shanshi sect in Aoli. The other got lost, was found by a merchant family fleeing persecution, and was carried up to Aurumshi (the Sky Cities). This lost child's bloodline eventually leads to Aria.

**Isenholt** (The Frozen Watch)
- Scandinavian + Mongolian blend. The Wall. Beast-bonded warriors.
- Born in winter, live in winter. Guard the mountain passes. Six clans based on animal bonds (not blood).
- Deep culture built around: Beast Bonding rite at 12, the Choosing (leadership combat), Marriage Fires, God-touched women, the Volugan (maiden priestesses/archivists).
- See separate Isenholt memory file for full detail.

**Ardmere** (The Farmlands)
- English/French inspiration. Peasants and slaves. A powder keg.
- Where druid-blooded people were enslaved after the purge. They make flowers grow and fields produce abundance without knowing it's magic in the blood.
- Like the French Revolution building slowly. The dam will break.

**Tyramare** (The Coastal Hub)
- Mediterranean -- Venice, Rome, Athens, Alexandria, Highgarden.
- Home of guilds and the empty Bannric throne. Seven guild seats control the province.
- Water gardens, waterways, islands connected to mainland. Beauty hiding corruption.
- See separate Tyramare memory file for full detail.

**Maramir** (The Steppes and Desert)
- Nomadic tribes. Mirage and illusion magic. Always moving.
- Ruled by the Shadow Prince (Yue'an) -- no one knows what the ruler looks like.
- The other provinces hire them for dirty work. They work in shadow and create illusion.

### 3. Solisopia (The Bridge)
- **Inspiration:** Roman/Byzantine empires. Sodom and Gomorrah. Jeweled rot.
- **Feel:** A land of excess. Pleasure, drink, idol worship, money worship. Glitters like jewels but is a pit.
- **Origin:** A culture that fused Aoli (Old World) and Caerndrath traditions into one.
- **Key detail:** The Water Twin runs a pleasure house here. Liyin Aurelian was raised here.
- **Corruption face:** Excess and indulgence

### 4. Aurumshi (The Sky Cities)
- **Inspiration:** Victorian steampunk + Asian twist. Gears, steam, metal everywhere.
- **Name:** Aurum (Latin gold) + Shi (iron). West meets East.
- **Origin:** These are the PEOPLE WHO BLAMED THE GODS. They fled from their choices and outlawed magic entirely.
- **Dark secret:** The cities FLOAT using blood magic -- the darkest magic. People born with magic are harvested. The ruling class knows; citizens are kept in a fog that makes them forget.
- **Key detail:** Minhui hides here. The prophecy child (Aria) was born here. The blood magic fog slides off Aria because her AuDHD brain refuses to be smoothed down.
- **Corruption face:** Ignorance and avoidance

## How Evil Manifests Across Cultures

Yueli's corruption wears a different face in each realm:
- **Aoli:** Chaos -- raw, ancient, elemental corruption (Shimo Sect)
- **Caerndrath:** Greed and blood -- in the hearts of men
- **Solisopia:** Excess and indulgence
- **Aurumshi:** Ignorance and avoidance
