## Tyramare Overview

- Mediterranean inspiration: Venice, Rome, Athens, Alexandria, Highgarden
- Capital city is on the coast, islands connected by waterways (like Venice). Province extends to mainland.
- Home of the EMPTY BANNRIC THRONE -- sits in the council chamber. The guild masters meet in its shadow.
- The throne is still there as a sign -- the guilds hold meetings in the room where it sits, treating it like furniture. Their arrogance.
- Province name =/= city name. City name still TBD.

## Seven Guild Districts

| # | District | Domain | How Seat is Claimed |
|---|----------|--------|-------------------|
| 1 | Spice Harbour | Trade & Merchants, land and sea routes | Voted by merchant families (votes can be bought) |
| 2 | Marble Quarter | Banking & Finance, storing/hoarding money | Matriarchal inheritance -- oldest daughter of the first family. WOMEN run the money. Currently unstable: first lady is old, no daughter heir. Power shifting. |
| 3 | Iron Quarter | Military, patrols, blacksmiths, horse breeders | Death battle. Military families put up a champion. Fight to the death. Last standing fights the current head. |
| 4 | The Veil | Pleasure district, opiates, poison in drinks | Given by the OTHER five seats. Changes every two years. Holder clings to a glass throne. |
| 5 | Aurumvale | Nobility, marble villas, magic flaunted openly | Inherited -- same family since the Bannric fall. Highest noble house below Bannric. The ONLY name kept from bygone days. |
| 6 | Stillhaven / The Silence Below | Underground: taverns, gambling, secrets bought, lives sold | Killed for. Current holder is a NEW unknown woman -- sly, watchful, sinister. She is Liyin's eyes, ears, and mouth on the council. Liyin now has a seat at the table. |
| 7 | The Solace | The Solenthera faith, the Entheras's domain | "God-given." Public believes one person has sat on it for centuries -- divine immortality. Actually a Shi Sect fragment-carrier. |

### Stillhaven vs The Silence Below
- **Stillhaven** (above): Taverns, dice, laughter, home for common folk. Still water that runs deep.
- **The Silence Below** (beneath): Where Tyramare buries what it doesn't want found. The faith's real money, secrets, bodies. Where Yueli is strongest. People go down and don't come back.

### Aurumvale
- Nobility live above it all in marble villas. Magic baked into every aspect -- street lamps, air, gardens bloom year-round. Children are arrogant, demanding, cruel.
- Inherited from time immemorial. They are the least powerful of all seats but believe they are the most.

## The Solenthera Faith

A public faith promising safety, guidance, and prosperity. Rolling, musical name with a sharp edge. "A lullaby that hides a blade."

- **The Entheras** -- the supreme leader. Pope figure. Almost always the deciding vote on the council. When he speaks, all is quiet. He is the power behind the curtain of corruption. He KNOWS the faith is a tool. He is a follower of Yueli. This has been building for CENTURIES -- a slow poison.
- **The Oranthi** -- temple runners. The daily faces. Kind, approachable. The ones who greet you at the door and walk you to the offering bowl.
- The religion stems from the Shimo Sect in Aoli. It was PLANTED in Tyramare.

## The Two Cults

### The Umbrathi (Shadow Prayer) -- Yueli's Cult
- Dark cult in the catacombs. Hands reaching. A mother humming a lullaby in the dark.
- Connected to Yueli's corruption. Most people don't believe it exists.
- Sits against the Solenthera like shadow against light.

### The Verenthae -- Yunsheng's Resistance
- Flowing and gentle, like a kiss. Soothing, peaceful, full of love and care.
- The good cult. Connected to Yunsheng's memory and balance.
- An underground resistance against the Solenthera's corruption.

## The Shimo Network

The Shimo Sect has planted operatives in ALL major hubs:
- **Aoli** -- the Shi Sect itself (the Avatar)
- **Tyramare** -- the Entheras (the faith)
- **Aurumshi** -- the Veiled Advisor (blood magic)
- **Solisopia** -- the Smiling Blade (pleasure quarter)
- The other guild masters do NOT know they are serving Yueli's agenda.
