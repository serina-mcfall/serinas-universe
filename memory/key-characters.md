## The Parents

### Zhuohuo (Chasing Fire)
- The Bannric heir. True heir to the empty throne of Caerndrath.
- A phoenix god (Fengzhe) is buried inside him, leaking power that damages his health.
- Stern by nature (life made him that way). Conflicted. Second-guesses himself. Has a temper when provoked -- hard to control. Solumn.
- He loved Minhui (thought she was a peasant in Ardmere). Married her. They lived in peace until war broke out. He was hunted again and they got separated. He went to Aoli; she continued to Aurumshi.
- Does NOT know Aria exists. Does not know Minhui was a goddess.
- Appearance: Strong jaw. Dark eyes with weight. Handsome but weathered. Long wild hair catching golden light. Scar under his eye. Traditional Chinese robes in dark tones.
- His dilemma: artifacts exist that can either seal Fengzhe or boost cultivation to let the god emerge. Does he seal the god and keep his life? Or surrender his body and leave gracefully?
- He remembers everything after death and rebirth -- anger and regret.
- The fire stone: requires his life as sacrifice for rebirth. He dies and is reborn with all memories.

### Minhui (Bright Wisdom)
- Nine-tailed celestial sky fox goddess. Spirit. Wenji's sister.
- Hid in Aurumshi for centuries telling stories, keeping old truths alive.
- Ethereal beauty with suppressed power. Small in stature. Green-gold eyes. Wisdom that sees all. Presence is peace and balance.
- She knew the prophecy. She fell in love with Zhuohuo genuinely. Discovered pregnancy on the road to Aurumshi. Left Aria at an orphanage to protect her. Never told Zhuohuo.
- She feels every death in the sky cities -- agonising for a spirit goddess.
- She watches Aria from afar (the image of Minhui watching Aria walk through a crowd with her bird).

## The Divine Guardians

### The Wind Serpent (Female)
- Yanlong's gift to protect sleeping Yunsheng
- Luminescent, lit from within. Long black hair tipped in purples and blue (like bleeding ink). Small in stature. Air of great power and greater wisdom.
- Speaks in metaphors -- when she speaks. Has been alone for centuries.
- Takes human form when the waking begins, leaves her cave for the Yan sect.
- Mental connection to the Koi -- they've been best friends across centuries despite never meeting.

### The Koi Fish Guardian (Male)
- Yunsheng's gift to protect sleeping Yanlong
- Playful, always cracking jokes. Inner strength not to be underestimated. Calculating but it doesn't show. Patient. Strength lies in patience.
- Takes human form too. Young-looking, deceptively soft. Carries a fan (weapon of scholars/schemers).
- Mental connection to the Wind Serpent -- only company for each other for centuries.

## The Immortal Twins

### The Earth Twin (Male)
- Son of Yanlong and Yunsheng. Immortal.
- Hiding in Braedun as the original druid. Taught the people of the crags the old ways.
- He is the source of the entire druidic tradition in Caerndrath.
- Has been walking among mortals for centuries.

### The Water Twin (Female)
- Daughter of Yanlong and Yunsheng. Immortal. Born of two: water and stone. Twin sibling to the Earth Twin.
- Hiding in Solisopia as the madam of a pleasure house.
- Has quietly guided Liyin Aurelian, helped the other women raise him. He grew up in the pleasure house, raised by the Twin and the other women.
- In a small house down an alleyway in Solisopia lives a hidden family that still remembers and carries on the old traditions. They harbour a bloodline that is just as sacred as Yunsheng's -- born of two, water and stone, descended from the divine twins. The Water Twin guards them.
