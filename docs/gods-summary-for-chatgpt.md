# THE GODS OF AVALÚNE — Cosmology Summary
*(Compact reference — paste this into ChatGPT or any AI writing partner so it has the same background.)*

---

## THE PRIMORDIALS (the eldest)

**Mùjīn** (暮金, "Sunset Gold") — Father. **Fate.** A golden dragon. The quiet certainty under everything. Sees all, knows all. Plans across centuries.

**Yuèlì** (月丽, "Moon Beauty") — Mother. **Chaos.** A black nine-tailed fox. Born as life and beauty incarnate. **Slowly corrupted** by time, separation, and humanity's greed and ambition. *Not inherently evil.* The saga's central truth: she is a corrupted mother who needs **SAVING**, not destroying.

---

## THE ELDEST CHILD (Gen 2)

**Yínhé** (银河, "Silver River" — also the Chinese name for the Milky Way). Eternally 12 years old. Eons old. Looks like a child. Domain: **youth, ingenuity, flow.** Born ~1,000 years before her younger siblings. The eight Sacred Trees were planted as her birth-gift.

**She stole Mùjīn's spool of fate-strings** (gold threads that bind predestined couples) and went on a spree tying chaotic love-bonds across the world. She is the unwitting mechanism of the Prophecy — Aria + Yuè'àn's romance is HER string. Her father knows she stole it. He lets her keep it because her strings are *working.* She is the closest thing Avalúne has to Mùjīn and Yuèlì being together again.

---

## THE FIVE YOUNGER GODS (Gen 2 — born ~5,000 years after Yínhé)

1. **Yánlóng** (Rock Dragon) — Earth, Wind, Forest. **Sleeping**; his body IS the Dragon's Spine mountain range. Curled around the root-heart of the Eight Trees, guarding it. Married to Yúnshēng. His tears = white "God's Tears" ivy on Aoli's peaks.

2. **Yúnshēng** (Cloud Born) — Water, Peace, Balance. **Sleeping** in Aoli. Married to Yánlóng. The rivers sing with her.

3. **Wénjī** — Wind & Music. Nine-tailed wind fox. Plays the guqin. **Exiled, wandering.** Patron of the Wen Sect in Aoli.

4. **Fèngzhé** — Fire. A fenghuang (phoenix). **Hidden inside the Bannric heir Zhúhuǒ** (Aria's father). A fragment of him is also lodged in Aurum's paulownia tree — the anchor that makes his return possible.

5. **Mínhuì** (明慧, "Bright Wisdom") — Spirit, Soul. Nine-tailed sky fox. **Aria's biological mother.** Hides in Aurum as a quiet white-haired noblewoman cataloguing scrolls in the Aurum Archival, secretly tending the corrupted paulownia tree. *Has not spoken to Aria in 16 years.*

---

## THE TWINS (Gen 3 — children of Yánlóng + Yúnshēng)

Born before their parents went to sleep. Immortal, walking among mortals for centuries. They share a **mind-link** and operate as one command structure across the continent.

- **Badan / Yánzǐ** ("Stone Child") — the **Earth Twin.** Hides in Braedun as the original druid; source of the entire druidic tradition of Caerndrath.
- **Lady Pearl / Yúnzhū** ("Cloud Pearl") — the **Water Twin.** Hides in Solis as the madam of a pleasure house that is secretly a fortress, financial empire, and spy network — built around the Mulberry Pillar Tree in her courtyard.

Their roots meet in an underground web. At the heart sits **THE SEED** — Mùjīn and Yuèlì's uncorrupted love for Yínhé, crystallised eternally. It is Yuèlì's *only uncorrupted piece.* Yánlóng sleeps curled around it. **The corruption is hunting the seed.** If it reaches it, the only mechanism to heal Yuèlì is destroyed. The Seed = the cosmic stake of the entire saga.

---

## THE PROPHECY

Mùjīn foretold a child born in a place where no magic dwells, with strong magic roots, but the world will fear them. When the child is born: the gods stir, the trees dance, the rivers sing, the mountains watch — and beneath the seal, *the evil stirs knowing its time has come.*

That child is **Aria** — daughter of Mínhuì + Zhúhuǒ. Born in Aurum (the no-magic city). Carries phoenix-fire through her father's blood. **The Hope-tree of Aurum is her tree.** When she heals it, she heals her own birthplace.

---

## THE CENTRAL THEME

Most fantasy says *defeat the evil.* This saga says **save her.** Find the mother under the darkness. Heal what humanity broke. The journey is to redeem Yuèlì, not destroy her.

---

## THE SHI SECT (the antagonist network)

An ancient order infiltrating every kingdom, attempting to free Yuèlì from her seal. They believe darkness should reign. Their inner circle carries **five fragments of Yuèlì's essence.** When all fragments unite, she is freed — but they are working from a corrupted version of her, not her truth.

Their **High Chancellor in Aurum is Guì** — also Aoli's "Guì Xī" — a single ancient witch who has worn many faces for centuries and binds both sagas under one infiltrator.
